Reds Hall of Famers

The Reds have had a total of members 39 inducted into the Major League Hall of fame and approximately 10 individuals who are primarily recognized as Cincinnati Reds. Their are also four managers who have managed the Reds and one broadcaster.

Most Significant Members

The most significant Reds that have made the Hall of Fame include:

    Johhny Bench
    Barry Larkin
    Joe Morgan
    Tony Perez
    Sparky Anderson
    Marty Brenaman
